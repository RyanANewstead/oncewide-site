Help with FrameOnce

Support

FrameOnce is built around a simple workflow: record one clip, let the app prepare the framing, choose an aspect ratio, then save or share the export.

Contact FrameOnce support

Email frameonce.support@gmail.com. Include your iPhone model, iOS version, FrameOnce version and a brief description of what happened. Do not attach private recordings unless specifically requested and you are comfortable sharing them.

Common solutions

  • The camera or microphone is unavailableOpen iPhone Settings, find FrameOnce and confirm Camera and Microphone access are enabled.
  • An export will not save to PhotosOpen iPhone Settings, find FrameOnce and confirm the app is allowed to add photos. Also check that the iPhone has enough free storage.
  • The person is not kept in frameUse good lighting, keep the person reasonably visible and avoid having several similar-looking people repeatedly cross paths. FrameOnce 1.0 is designed primarily to follow people rather than arbitrary products, pets or balls.
  • Processing is slowLong clips and older devices take more time. Keep FrameOnce open while it prepares the framing and exports.
  • The iPhone becomes warmVideo analysis and export are demanding tasks. Allow the device to cool before processing another long clip if needed.
  • The recording or export is sidewaysInstall the latest FrameOnce update and retry. When contacting support, include the orientation used and whether the phone was rotated before or during recording.
  • The app closes unexpectedlyReopen FrameOnce and try a shorter recording. Include the approximate clip length and what the app was doing when it closed in your support message.

Before contacting support

  1. Install the latest available FrameOnce version.
  2. Confirm your iPhone has enough free storage.
  3. Restart FrameOnce and retry the recording once.
  4. Note the aspect ratio and orientation involved.

Privacy

FrameOnce performs its core tracking and export processing on your iPhone. Read the FrameOnce Privacy Policy for details.

Platform recommendations

Names and posting suggestions shown in FrameOnce are general guidance only. Third-party platforms may change their accepted or preferred video formats. FrameOnce is not affiliated with or endorsed by those platforms.

Response time

FrameOnce is an independently operated app. Support messages are handled as promptly as practical, but immediate or around-the-clock support is not guaranteed.

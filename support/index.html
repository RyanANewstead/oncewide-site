Help with OnceWide

Support

OnceWide is built around a simple workflow: record one clip, let the app prepare the framing, choose an aspect ratio, then save or share the export.

Contact OnceWide support

Email support.oncewide@gmail.com. Include your iPhone model, iOS version, OnceWide version and a brief description of what happened. Do not attach private recordings unless specifically requested and you are comfortable sharing them.

Common solutions

  • The camera or microphone is unavailableOpen iPhone Settings, find OnceWide and confirm Camera and Microphone access are enabled.
  • An export will not save to PhotosOpen iPhone Settings, find OnceWide and confirm the app is allowed to add photos. Also check that the iPhone has enough free storage.
  • The person is not kept in frameUse good lighting, keep the person reasonably visible and avoid having several similar-looking people repeatedly cross paths. OnceWide 1.0 is designed primarily to follow people rather than arbitrary products, pets or balls.
  • Processing is slowLong clips and older devices take more time. Keep OnceWide open while it prepares the framing and exports.
  • The iPhone becomes warmVideo analysis and export are demanding tasks. Allow the device to cool before processing another long clip if needed.
  • The recording or export is sidewaysInstall the latest OnceWide update and retry. When contacting support, include the orientation used and whether the phone was rotated before or during recording.
  • The app closes unexpectedlyReopen OnceWide and try a shorter recording. Include the approximate clip length and what the app was doing when it closed in your support message.

Before contacting support

  1. Install the latest available OnceWide version.
  2. Confirm your iPhone has enough free storage.
  3. Restart OnceWide and retry the recording once.
  4. Note the aspect ratio and orientation involved.

Privacy

OnceWide performs its core tracking and export processing on your iPhone. Read the OnceWide Privacy Policy for details.

Platform recommendations

Names and posting suggestions shown in OnceWide are general guidance only. Third-party platforms may change their accepted or preferred video formats. OnceWide is not affiliated with or endorsed by those platforms.

Response time

OnceWide is an independently operated app. Support messages are handled as promptly as practical, but immediate or around-the-clock support is not guaranteed.

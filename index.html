Frequently asked questions

The important details.

FrameOnce is intentionally narrow. It does one useful job without becoming a general-purpose editor.

Is FrameOnce a subscription?

No. The plan for version 1.0 is a single, low-cost App Store purchase with no recurring payment.

Does FrameOnce add a watermark?

No. Exports are clean and retain the recording's audio.

Does my video leave my iPhone?

FrameOnce performs its core detection, tracking, reframing and export processing on your iPhone. It does not upload your recordings to a FrameOnce server.

What can FrameOnce track?

Version 1.0 is designed primarily to follow one clearly visible person. It is not yet an arbitrary object, pet or ball tracker.

Which formats does it create?

FrameOnce exports 9:16 vertical, 16:9 landscape, 4:5 portrait and 1:1 square video.

Is FrameOnce an editor?

No. It deliberately avoids timelines, filters, templates and other editing complexity. The workflow is record, process, choose and export.

Which devices will be supported?

The initial release is planned for compatible iPhones running iOS 18 or later.
